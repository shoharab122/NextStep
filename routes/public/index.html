Why Applications Go Wrong

Students select a destination without confirming whether their profile actually fits.

Rushed Decisions for Intakes

Applications are hurried to meet deadlines, reducing the quality of preparation.

Visa Risk Not Explained Clearly

Potential risks are ignored instead of being properly assessed and addressed early.

Profile Reviewed Too Late

Academic background, study gaps, and documents are checked after key decisions are made.
